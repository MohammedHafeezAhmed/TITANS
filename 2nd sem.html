Learnt in first semester! :



  1. Empathize – Observe, gain insights, and understand the problem.

  2. Define – Frame the problem using “How might we...” statements.

  3. Ideate – Brainstorming to generate ideas and understand the value of the problem.

  4. Prototype – Create a Proof of Concept (POC), then a low-fidelity prototype, and eventually a Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

  5. Test – Evaluate the prototype and gather feedback.






Stakeholder Mapping:
Creator – The one who creates the problem, either directly or indirectly.
Victim – The one who is impacted by the problem.






Design Thinking is a human-centered problem-solving approach.






Empathy Map – Helps empathize with the user by identifying what they:
See Feel Think Hear Do Fear Experience as pain or gain.



Why Web – Used for root cause analysis.



HMW (How Might We) – Focuses on action, purpose, and stakeholder empathy.

  2. Desirability (Human-centered)

  3. Viability (Business/Financial)

  4. Feasibility (Technological)
