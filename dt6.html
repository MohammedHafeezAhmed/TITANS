Design Thinking - Class 6 : How Might We



Our team, 'TITANS' has begun working on a task focused on 'How Might We' questions. This involves framing questions using 'How Might We Statement' exploring possible solutions, and understanding the reasons behind them. During the session, Vignesh Sir explained the process in detail, helping us grasp the concept more effectively. With his guidance, we are now brainstorming different ideas, discussing various possibilities, and identifying key problems. This activity is not only enhancing our problem-solving skills but also encouraging us to think more creatively and critically.

At first, it seemed difficult, but step by step, we figured it out and got it done. Challenges tested us, but we stayed determined and kept moving forward. In the end, all the effort paid off, and we successfully completed the task.
