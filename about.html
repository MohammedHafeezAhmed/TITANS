Team Titans

We are a team of six—three girls and three boys—working together to develop a responsive website that digitalizes attendance and marks management in government schools. Our team is responsible for different aspects of the project, from designing the interface and developing the frontend to writing content, and ensuring quality. We are focused on making the website efficient, user-friendly, and accessible, ensuring that teachers can easily record, update, and retrieve student data. By combining our skills, we aim to create an advanced solution that simplifies record-keeping and reduces manual effort.

One of our team member {KHUSHI RAVI} plays a key role in coordinating tasks and refining the user experience. She ensures that the website is visually appealing, easy to navigate, and optimized for all devices. In addition to managing design, she actively contributes to **team discussions, testing features, and making improvements based on feedback. Her dedication and attention to detail help shape the project into a well-structured and polished platform.
